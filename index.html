About Me

I'm Olakunde Samuel O., a self-taught React Developer based in Okitipupa, Nigeria. I'm currently focused on frontend development, having progressed from basic web technologies to React. My journey in tech started during my computer science studies, and I've been continuously learning and building projects since then

Why I Want to Be a Front-End Developer and How HNG Will Help Me Achieve My Goals

Journey

My path into frontend development began unexpectedly. While in a period of uncertainty, I observed a friend venturing into web development, which sparked my curiosity. This coincided with my computer science studies, which I initially viewed as the natural entry point into tech. Though this assumption was premature, it became the catalyst for my development journey. As noted on HNG's React.js developers page , many successful developers start with such exploratory steps before finding their true passion in frontend development. Through self-directed learning, I've progressed from basic concepts to React development, currently working on projects including a weather application and an e-commerce site. While these projects showcase my growth, they've also illuminated the areas where I need to develop further expertise.

Challenges

The self-taught path hasn't been without its obstacles. As highlighted in HNG's JavaScript developers section, strong foundational knowledge is crucial, yet this was precisely my initial struggle. I found myself drowning in tutorials, often learning what felt like "gibberish" without grasping the fundamental principles of the languages. The lack of structured learning led to confusion about essential concepts, and I often missed the core essence of what I was studying. However, through persistence and continuous effort, I managed to navigate through this confusion. Each challenge became a stepping stone, helping me find my own path in frontend development. Though not perfect, these struggles have shaped my understanding and approach to learning.

Goals

  • Mastering ARIA attributes for better accessibility implementation
  • Solidifying my JavaScript foundations
  • Perfecting React development skills
  • Expanding into TypeScript and Node.js

The structured, project-based approach of HNG aligns perfectly with my learning needs. While I acknowledge that learning in tech is endless, my focus is on mastering React and its ecosystem as a foundation for growth.
